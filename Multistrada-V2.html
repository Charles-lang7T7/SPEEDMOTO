Designed from scratch

The new Multistrada V2 stems from a total reinterpretation of the crossover, concept, with a design that transmits dynamism and lightness. Every detail has been revised, for a bike that stands out not just for its aesthetic but also for the perfect integration of functionality and style.

New V2 engine

The new V2 engine is the lightest twin-cylinder that Ducati has ever produced. Its lightness significantly contributes to the reduction in overall weight of the Multistrada V2, to the benefit of handling and riding comfort.

Completely new chassis

The new Multistrada V2 is designed to offer an even safer and more engaging riding experience. With the new aluminium monocoque frame, double-sided swingarm and trellis subframe, the bike perfectly balances lightness, precision and handling.

Advanced electronics

The advanced electronics of the new Multistrada V2 optimise every aspect of riding, guaranteeing greater performance, safety and total control in every situation.

The easiest Multistrada ever

The new Multistrada V2 offers the perfect balance of lightness, handling and comfort. The slim design and improved ergonomics make for smooth and accessible riding, while the new frame and V2 engine ensure stability and responsiveness. Every detail, from the seat position to the customisation options, is designed to adapt to your riding style.
