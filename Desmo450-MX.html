In pursuit of performance and functionality.

Centro Stile Ducati has designed an MX bike with clean, flowing and taut surfaces, making the Desmo450 MX immediately recognizable as a Ducati. The design guarantees the rider maximum freedom of movement and is purely functional in terms of movement in the seat, grip, responsiveness and speed.

Desmo450: the revolution starts here.

The Desmo450 MX was built around its beating heart: the 63.5 hp Desmo450 engine, the first four-stroke single-cylinder with desmodromic timing in the history of motocross. Designed before anything else, it was the engine that gave shape to the bike itself, redefining the concept.

First-rate chassis: stability, grip and control with which to push to the max.

Every element of the Desmo450 MX chassis has been developed to offer precision, control and responsiveness by harnessing a select combination di materials and technical partners.

Ducati electronics at the service of performance.

The Desmo450 MX brings Ducati electronics excellence to motocross. A technological package that, for the first time in MX, introduces adjustable Traction Control that can effectively measure rear wheel slip on the ground, intervening directly based on the loss of grip.

Born to compete.

The new Ducati Desmo450 MX was developed in races, for racing. The 63,5 hp 450 engine with Desmodromic timing, the aluminium frame weighing 8,96 kg and the premium components offer everything a rider needs to compete well at any level.

Create your Desmo450 MX with Ducati Performance accessories.

With the Ducati Performance accessories catalogue, the Ducati Desmo450 MX can be customised with the same components used by the Factory Team competing in the MXGP World Championship with Jeremy Seewer and Mattia Guadagnini. Explore the customisation options in the configurator.
