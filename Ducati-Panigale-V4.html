Fast as the wind

Aerodynamics evolves to achieve maximum integration between aesthetic function and aerodynamic performance. A work that has seen designers and Ducati Corse technicians come together to develop solutions to reduce drag while maintaining a high vertical load.

Designed around the rider

The main changes regard the contact points between motorcycle and rider and the position in the saddle. Tank, footpegs and seat fit area have been completely revised with the aim of heightening control in the most important phases of track riding and allowing the rider to fully exploit the performance of the Panigale V4 both over a single lap and during an extended session.

Mission racetrack

Based on Ducati Corse experience, the chassis layout aims to improve track performance, or rather faster lap times that are achieved with less effort and can be more consistently replicated by both amateurs and professionals. Thanks to solutions such as the Ducati Hollow Symmetrical Swingarm and Front Frame with reduced lateral stiffness, the new Panigale V4 is quicker through the most delicate phases of track riding.

Technological re-evolution

The electronic solutions integrated into the new Panigale V4 offer the rider an even more safe and sophisticated superbike that can amplify the riding ability of every rider.

The heart of performance

The new Panigale V4 is powered by the latest generation Desmosedici Stradale. The MotoGP-derived engine evolves to conform with strict Euro 5+ standards and noise thresholds, achieving 216 hp of maximum power and dropping 1 kg in weight.

Expand the limits of customisation

Ducati allows each rider to customise their Panigale V4. Riders can pick from a range of Ducati Performance accessories to enhance performance, aesthetics, and versatility. And by joining the exclusive Ducati Unica customisation programme, a rider can live the dream of creating their very own V4.
